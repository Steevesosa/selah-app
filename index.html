Use Selah better
Everything you need to know
1
Just talk or type. No grammar, no order. Say it like you are venting to a friend.
2
Tap the chips. Working, overwhelmed, things from yesterday. Selah uses that to sort better.
3
Hit the button. Selah pulls priorities, builds your day, and pushes anything unrealistic to tomorrow.
4
Add what matters. Tap "Add to tasks" on any item including tomorrow items. Star the ones that are highest priority.
5
Check things off. Unchecked tasks carry forward. If something sits for 7 days Selah will flag it and ask what you want to do.
Tips for better results
Include everything. Even stuff you have been putting off for weeks. Selah decides today vs later.
Mention urgency. "Due Friday" or "been putting this off a week" moves things up in priority.
Not everything fits today. Selah will push things to tomorrow if your plate is too full. That is the point.
Use your keyboard mic. Talk instead of type. Selah handles spelling and grammar.
